Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience?

No prior experience is required. Our workshop is beginner-friendly!

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable clothing that you don't mind getting a little messy.

How can I register?

You can register using the form on our website. Simply fill in your details and we'll get back to you with the next steps.

Will I get to keep the pottery I make?

Yes! You will take home your creations after they've been fired and glazed.
